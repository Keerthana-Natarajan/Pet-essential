About us

The Drools Journey: Passion for Pet Nutrition

Drools was born out of a simple yet powerful belief: pets deserve the best nutrition possible. With a team of dedicated veterinarians and nutrition experts, we’ve crafted scientifically balanced meals to ensure optimal health and happiness for pets everywhere. From sourcing premium ingredients to maintaining rigorous quality standards, Drools is committed to delivering nutritious, delicious, and safe food for your beloved pets. Today, we’re trusted by millions of pet parents worldwide who share our passion for giving their pets the best.

The Mindful & Deliberate Choice We Make

We only use quality ingredients responsibly sourced from farmers and ranchers we know and trust. Our MEALS are 90-95% chicken, organ, bone, and our animal proteins have no added hormones or antibiotics.

  • Digestible Protein

  • No Added Hormones
    Or Antibiotics

  • No By-Products

Quality You Can Trust

At Drools, we are dedicated to providing the highest quality pet food, ensuring every ingredient meets rigorous safety and nutritional standards. Our commitment to excellence begins with sourcing premium ingredients from trusted suppliers and continues through our meticulous manufacturing processes. We believe that pets deserve only the best, and we stand behind our products with a promise of quality and safety.

Committed to Pet Health and Nutrition

At Drools, our mission is to provide pets with top-quality nutrition for a healthier, happier life. We believe every pet deserves food that supports their well-being and vitality. With premium ingredients and veterinarian-approved formulas, we craft meals pets love and owners trust, ensuring a future where pets thrive.

Our Vision for Healthier Pets

At Drools, our vision is to provide pets with the nutrition they need for a healthy, vibrant life. We create scientifically-backed, high-quality food that promotes well-being, longevity, and happiness. Our goal is to help pets thrive, one meal at a time..
